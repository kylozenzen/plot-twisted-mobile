How To Play

The Goal

Read the twisted plot summary and guess the correct movie title by selecting letters on the keyboard.

Game Rules

  • Standard Game: Choose a category and play 5, 10, or 15 questions.
  • Strikes: You have 3 strikes total. Wrong letter = 1 strike.
  • Scoring: Each correct answer is worth 100 points.

Daily Challenge

Unique set of 10 questions each day. One attempt. Share your score!

Game Controls

  • Skip: Forfeit the question and move on.
  • Settings: Themes, sounds, game length.
